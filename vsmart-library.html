Installation Guide

  1. Setup Compiler: Download Hammer5Tools and open any addon. This action automatically configures the CS2 compiler to support .vsmart files.
  2. Deploy File: Copy your downloaded .vsmart file into any of your content subfolders (e.g., models, smartprops, or even a custom one). It doesn't matter which folder you use!
